About Us

Company History

Established in 2017, Lady Leah’s Transport delivers across Johannesburg and Pretoria with trust and efficiency. With a strong reputation for fast delivery, cost-effectiveness, and trustworthiness, LLT has built long-term relationships with individuals and businesses seeking dependable logistical solutions.

Lady Leah’s Transport has grown from a small family-owned business into a trusted logistics partner serving Johannesburg and Pretoria. We pride ourselves on reliability, affordability, and a commitment to exceptional customer service.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide affordable, secure, and timely transport solutions that connect people and businesses with efficiency and trust.

Vision: To become Gauteng’s leading transport and logistics company, recognized for innovation, customer satisfaction, and reliability.

Core Values

  • Integrity in every delivery
  • Customer-first approach
  • Innovation in logistics solutions
  • Commitment to safety
