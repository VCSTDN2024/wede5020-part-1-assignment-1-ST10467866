Who We Are

Established in 2017, Lady Leah’s Transport (LLT) has become one of Gauteng’s most trusted logistics partners. We started with a simple goal — to bridge the gap between fast delivery and personal care. Today, we deliver hundreds of packages daily across Johannesburg, Pretoria, and surrounding areas with precision and passion.

From humble beginnings with a single delivery van to a growing fleet of vehicles, our success has been driven by our commitment to reliability, affordability, and excellence in customer service.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide affordable, secure, and timely transport solutions that connect people and businesses with efficiency and trust.

Vision: To become Gauteng’s leading transport and logistics company, recognized for innovation, customer satisfaction, and reliability.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We believe in transparency and honesty in every delivery we make.
  • Customer-Centric: Every decision we make starts with understanding our clients’ needs.
  • Reliability: On-time, every time — it’s a promise we stand by.
  • Innovation: Embracing technology to make deliveries smarter and faster.
  • Sustainability: We strive to reduce our carbon footprint through optimized routes and eco-conscious practices.
